Landing Page: a polished elevator pitch, selected images, and a call to launch the application.

White as Snow is an interactive story for adults. Historical context, sociological analysis, and visual surprise mix with more traditional text-based narrative to shed light on the Grimm's classic tale. This interactive book is an exploration of new possibilities for stories in the digital world. Interaction design often hopes to be invisible, a means to deliver content rather than an end in itself. In this project, the graphic interactions are as exciting as they are functional. Reading White as Snow is an act of uncovering and creating, a new means of exercising the imagination.
